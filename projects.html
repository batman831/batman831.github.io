[RESEARCH] Firewall GOST

An educational game to teach firewall configuration and networking basics.

Developed as a part of Gamification of Security Training (GOST), IIT Delhi, Firewall is a game which intends to teach the players basics of packet filtering firewall configurations along with networking basics. The motive of game is to equip player with even no background knowledge of networking with essentials of networking and need of firewall and configuring simple firewalls.

Our research in this area has also led to a soon to be published paper

You can play the game right on your browser. Expected playtime is ~ 60 minutes

Brick Break

Brick Break is a spinoff of classic DX-Ball game with improvised graphics and features and a level builder built right into the game.

The objective is to destroy all bricks in a level without letting the ball sink. You get only 3 chances. There are also loads of powerup to help you with your task but there are also some negative powerups among them.

The game currently features 11 pre-designed levels (more coming soon) but you have access to level builder right in the game, so feel free to let your creativity and fun out.

Available on PC and Android

Sounds : A Blind Maze

Echolocate to win and survive. Available on Play Store and PC.

Created in Collaboration with my friend Shubham, Sounds ABM is my first android game. It features an unique game mechanism, you percieve the surrounding with the help of echos! After all, you are out exploring in a dark cave full of fearsome creatures with acute hearing bent on eliminating any foreign creature.

"Sounds: A Blind Maze" follows the player in a first-person perspective as you navigate through a maze-like cave. The features of the cave can only be made known to you through sounds, which your extremely experienced ears can detect with such precision that the player visualizes it as light itself.
Beware! If the sound you create reaches the wrong ears, you will find yourself running for your life.

Levels of increasing complexity are arranged to test your gaming skills as a shooter as well as navigator. Get ready to have your nerves on edge with every step you take!.

Available on Play Store as beta release.

Brick Break

Brick Break is a spinoff of classic DX-Ball game with improvised graphics and features and a level builder built right into the game.

The objective is to destroy all bricks in a level without letting the ball sink. You get only 3 chances. There are also loads of powerup to help you with your task but there are also some negative powerups among them.

The game currently features 7 pre-designed levels (more coming soon) but you have access to level builder right in the game, so feel free to let your creativity and fun out.
